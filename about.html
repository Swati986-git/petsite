Caring for Pets Since 2010

PawsomeClinic was founded with a simple mission: to provide exceptional care for pets and peace of mind for their owners. Our state-of-the-art facility is equipped with the latest technology to ensure your pet receives the best possible care.

Our team of experienced veterinarians and staff are passionate about animal welfare and are committed to treating each pet as if they were our own. We understand that your pets are family members, and we strive to make their visit as comfortable and stress-free as possible.

Our History

The story of PawsomeClinic

2010

PawsomeClinic was founded by Dr. James Wilson with just two examination rooms and a small team of three staff members.

2013

Expanded our facility to include a surgical suite and added specialized services for exotic pets.

2016

Opened our pet boarding facilities and added grooming services to provide comprehensive care.

2020

Celebrated our 10th anniversary and renovated our clinic with state-of-the-art equipment.

Today

Continuing to grow and serve our community with a team of dedicated professionals and a commitment to excellence in pet healthcare.
